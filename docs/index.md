# Paletteer

**Beautiful color palettes for Google Sheets**

Paletteer is a Google Sheets add-on that helps you generate and manage harmonious color palettes directly in your spreadsheet.

## Features

- **Generate Palettes** - Create beautiful, harmonious color palettes with a single click
- **Lock Colors** - Keep colors you love while regenerating the rest
- **Apply to Cells** - Click to apply background colors, Shift+click for text colors
- **Save Favorites** - Build a personal library of your favorite colors
- **Import from Coolors** - Paste a Coolors.co URL to import existing palettes
- **Compact Mode** - Minimize the sidebar when you need more space

## How It Works

1. Open the add-on from **Extensions > Paletteer > Open Sidebar**
2. Click **Generate** to create a new palette
3. Click any color swatch to apply it to selected cells
4. Lock colors you want to keep, then generate again
5. Star your favorites to save them for later

## Privacy

Paletteer respects your privacy. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. All color generation happens locally, and your favorites are stored in your own Google account.
